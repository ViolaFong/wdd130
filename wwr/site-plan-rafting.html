Overview

Purpose

Our goals for this website are: 1) to market our white water rafting products/excursions, 2) to help visitors to the site feel the exitement they would have if they join us in white water rafting, and 3) to provide visitors with easy access to the information they need to choose and book an excursion.

Audience

Our audience is primarily: 1) Experienced rafters who want to challenge themselves, 2) Corporate groups and youth groups or other group entities who would enjoy the white water rafting experience as a team-building or adventure experience, 3) Healthy families and adults who enjoy adventure and can afford our products, including tourists, 4) Adults who can afford to give a white water rafting excursion as a gift, and 5) Youth who will convince their parents or leaders that this is something they ought to purchase. What do they want? Adventure, memories, team building, assurance that they can safely engage in a memory-making adventure where all the details and arrangements are taken care of for them. What are their unmet needs? Adventure, someone to take care of all the details for them, safety, experts to guide them through the experience, value for a bargain price. How will our audience access the site? Via desktop, labtop, and/or mobile device.
